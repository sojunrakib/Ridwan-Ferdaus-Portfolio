My Journey in Hadith Studies

Hadith Scholar & Islamic Researcher

I am Ridwan Ferdaus, an M.Phil researcher at Al-Azhar University, Cairo, with a deep passion for Hadith studies, particularly the science of isnād (chains of narration), rijāl (biographies of narrators), and ʿilal (hidden defects). A lifelong learner, I also enjoy exploring philosophy and psychology, always seeking to connect knowledge with understanding and practice.
